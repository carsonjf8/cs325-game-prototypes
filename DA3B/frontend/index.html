How to Play

The objective of the game is to have your team reach the goal number of kills before your opponents do it first.

Use WASD or the ARROW KEYS to move.

Use the MOUSE to look around.

Use SPACE or LEFT CLICK to shoot.

You only have a limited view, everything else will be black.

Killing your opponent will send them back to one of their respawn locations.

There are RED and YELLOW powerups, each will last for 10 seconds. The RED one will give you rapid fire and the YELLOW one will give you a larger flashlight.
